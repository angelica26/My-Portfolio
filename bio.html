BIO

A longstanding appreciation for exceptional design and technology has led me to become a designer for both print and web platforms. I deliver engaging, effective solutions by striving to deeply understand the visions and needs of my clients. For inspiration, I look to art, pop culture, nature, and current events.


I hold a BFA in graphic design from Georgian Court University and have completed an intensive full stack web development boot camp through Rutgers University. Working for retailers such as Whole Foods Market has allowed me to develop extensive customer service experience, strong communication skills, and creative problem solving abilities.
